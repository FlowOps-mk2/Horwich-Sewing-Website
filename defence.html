Why now

European defence is rearming: and sourcing closer to home

European defence spending reached roughly €864 billion in 2025, up 14% year on year, the sharpest rise since the Cold War, with equipment procurement policy increasingly favouring EU-based suppliers and resilient domestic supply chains.

Defence consumes large volumes of sewn technical textiles: protective and field garments, layering systems, covers and load-carrying equipment. As a manufacturer inside the EU and NATO, with a genuine protective-clothing pedigree · Horwich Sewing SK is well placed to support that demand.
