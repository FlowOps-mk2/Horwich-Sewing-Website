Compliance & accreditations

Everything a procurement or supply-chain team needs to qualify us, our certifications, standards, traceability, security and supply-chain resilience, in one place.

Accreditations

What we hold, and what's in progress

We believe in being straight with buyers: here is exactly where we stand, including the credentials we're actively working toward for defence and government supply chains.

ISO 9001:2015 Held

Quality management, certified by SGS since 2017 (cert. SK17/2706). Every garment inspected; full SAP traceability.

ISO 14001:2015 Held

Environmental management, certified by SGS since 2023 (cert. SK23/00000132).

Cyber Essentials In progress

Targeted to meet the MOD Cyber Security Model and DEFCON 658; Cyber Essentials Plus to follow for higher-risk contracts.

JOSCAR (Hellios) In progress

Joint Supply Chain Accreditation Register, the prequalification used across aerospace, defence and security.

AQAP 2110 Targeted

NATO quality assurance, building on our ISO 9001 system, for defence orders that require it.

NCAGE code Targeted

NATO Commercial and Government Entity code, enabling supply into MOD and NATO programmes.

Working toward a credential you need? Tell us your requirement, we'll confirm timelines.

Quality & traceability

Audited quality, end to end

Production runs under our EN ISO 9001 system with in-house inspection and sign-off before despatch. Our SAP ERP gives full batch traceability from fabric and trim through cutting, sewing and packing to delivery.

We manufacture under our own certifications and can be audited to your specific requirements, and arrange independent certification and testing for your end market where needed.

Security & resilience

Built to keep supplying

Information security

Working to the MOD Cyber Security Model; ready to handle OFFICIAL-SENSITIVE information and contract data appropriately.

Supply-chain resilience

Production across our Slovak facility, satellite capacity in Macedonia and a network of trusted outsourcers, second-source and surge capability for framework and call-off contracts.

Business continuity

Modular manufacturing across multiple sites underpins continuity of supply through demand spikes.

Provenance & sovereignty

European-made, UK-rooted

We are a UK-headquartered business manufacturing inside the EU and NATO, with UK jobs and management. That gives allied provenance, tariff-free EU access and full transparency over our supply chain.

Where a contract requires UK content or UK finishing, we're open to partnership and onshore-finishing arrangements, talk to us about your sovereignty requirements.
