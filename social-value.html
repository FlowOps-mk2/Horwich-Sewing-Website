More than manufacturing

Public-sector and prime buyers weight social value in their decisions. Here's how working with Horwich Sewing SK contributes — to economy, skills, environment and people.

Our contribution

Aligned to the Social Value Model

We help clients meet their own social-value commitments under the Procurement Act and Social Value Model, across the themes that matter.

Economic contribution

A UK-headquartered SME with UK management and jobs, manufacturing in the EU and NATO. Agile, owner-led decision-making and resilient, transparent supply chains that support local economies in both countries.

Skills & employment

We invest in and preserve specialist cut, sew, weld and technical skills — craft that takes years to build — and provide rewarding, skilled employment.

Environment

ISO 14001 certified, with measurable action: a 60% cut in single-use plastics, 80% less paper, biodegradable packaging and circular-design principles. See our approach →

People & community

Our owners' first principle is to make Horwich a rewarding, enjoyable place to work. We treat our team, suppliers and clients with the same honesty and care.
