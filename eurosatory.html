Protective-clothing heritage

Since 1988, including the complete PPE range for Kimberly-Clark. Chemical-protection and CBRN-type suits, fire-protective garments, field and combat clothing, covers and sewn kit.

Technical construction

All forms of sewing plus high-frequency and ultrasonic welding and taped liquid-barrier seams, with ISO 9001 inspection and full SAP batch traceability.
