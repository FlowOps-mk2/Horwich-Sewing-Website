Who we are

Horwich Sewing SK (“we”, “us”) is the data controller for personal data submitted through this website. Contact: info@horwichsewingsk.com.

What we collect

When you use our enquiry form we collect the details you provide, typically your name, company, email, phone number and the content of your enquiry. We do not collect special-category data and ask that you do not include it.

Why we use it & our lawful basis

We use your information solely to respond to your enquiry, provide quotations and manage our business relationship with you. Our lawful basis is our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries and, where applicable, taking steps to enter into a contract.

Sharing & retention

We do not sell your data. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate (for example email and IT providers) under appropriate safeguards. We keep enquiry data only as long as necessary for the purpose above and our legitimate business and legal needs.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, erase or restrict the use of your personal data, and to object to processing. To exercise any of these, email us at the address above. You may also complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

Cookies

This website uses only what is necessary to function and loads web fonts from a content-delivery network. If analytics or marketing cookies are added in future, we will request your consent and update this notice.
