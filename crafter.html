Crafter by Horwich

Workwear that moves you — durable, comfortable, fit-for-purpose garments designed with the people who wear them.

The mission

Great work starts here

Born in 2020 from the values of its parent company, Crafter Collection exists to create truly fit-for-purpose garments of the highest quality — and provide them at a great price. Pieces that are comfortable all day, fit well, and survive the everyday demands of industry professionals.

Through innovations in fabric technology, co-designing garments with the people who wear them, and our “Make Your Mark” campaign, Crafter is about building the confidence, respect and empowerment of individuals and businesses alike.

The innovation

Where innovation meets quality

In the search for fabrics offering both durability and stretch, Crafter chose CORDURA® NYCO — renowned for strength and durability — enhanced with MoveX® technology by Sapphire Mills. The result is workwear engineered to perform and built to last.
