Responsible sourcing & modern slavery

Honesty and integrity have defined us since 1988. That extends to how we treat people and source materials, across our own operations and our supply chain.

Modern slavery statement

Our commitment

Horwich Sewing SK has zero tolerance for slavery, forced or compulsory labour, child labour and human trafficking. We are committed to acting ethically and with integrity in all our business relationships, and to implementing effective systems and controls to ensure these practices are not taking place anywhere in our operations or supply chain.

Our business & supply chain

We manufacture technical and protective products at our facility in Slovakia, with satellite capacity and a network of trusted, long-standing outsourcers and material suppliers, predominantly within Europe. Our European supply base and direct, long-term supplier relationships give us strong visibility over working conditions.

Due diligence & controls

  • Supplier selection favouring established European partners we know well
  • Expectations set out to suppliers on labour standards and ethical conduct
  • Right-to-work and fair-employment checks within our own operations
  • Quality and site audits that also provide visibility of working conditions
  • A route for staff and partners to raise concerns confidentially

Ethical & responsible sourcing

We source fabrics, trims and components from reputable suppliers, increasingly favouring certified sustainable materials. Our ISO 14001 environmental management and our work toward circular-design principles sit alongside our social commitments, responsible sourcing means people and planet.
