Two ways to work with us

Fully factored or CMT

Fully factored

We take it all on, sourcing the material, developing the design and creating the complete garment, then delivering it to you finished and inspected. Ideal when you want a single partner from concept to delivery.

CMT: Cut, Make & Trim

You supply the materials; we cut, make and trim to your specification with precision and speed. Ideal when you control your own fabric supply.

How we ensure quality

Certified processes (ISO 9001 & 14001), in-house inspection, and end-to-end traceability through our SAP ERP system. We manufacture under our own certifications and can be audited to your specific requirements, including certification and testing where you need it.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between fully factored and CMT?

With fully factored production we source the materials, develop the design and make the complete garment, then deliver it finished and inspected. With CMT (cut, make and trim) you supply the materials and we cut, make and trim to your specification.

What manufacturing capabilities do you have in-house?

Design and tech packs, fabric and trim sourcing, pattern making and grading, computerised cutting, all forms of sewing, high-frequency and ultrasonic welding, tape and seam welding, embroidery, quality control and packaging.

How do you control quality and traceability?

Every product is inspected to ISO 9001, with full batch traceability through our SAP ERP system. We manufacture under our own certifications and can be audited to your specific requirements.

What are your typical lead times?

Typical lead times are around six weeks, though this depends on the product, fabric and volumes. Send us your specification and we will come back with a clear quote and lead time.

Can you scale up for large or surge orders?

Yes. Modular production lines and a network of trusted outsourcers let us scale individual teams or the whole operation, so we can handle large volumes and surge demand for framework and call-off contracts.
